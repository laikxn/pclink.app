Common Issues

PC shows as offline
Make sure the PCLink Agent is running in your Windows system tray (bottom-right of taskbar). Try right-clicking the tray icon and selecting Restart Agent. Check that both your phone and PC have internet access.

Wake on LAN not working
WoL requires a wired Ethernet connection and must be enabled in BIOS under Power Management settings. It does not work over Wi-Fi on most PCs. Your PC also needs to remain plugged into power.

Can't pair my device
Make sure the agent is running and showing a QR code. Codes expire after 5 minutes — if it's expired, right-click the tray icon and select Pair / Repair Device to generate a new one. Try entering the 6-digit code manually if the QR scan fails.

Commands are slow or not working
Check your internet connection on both your phone and PC. If the PC shows online but commands fail, try restarting the agent from the system tray.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

PCLink is designed with your privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell your personal data.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, device identifiers, usage analytics, crash reports, or any other personal information. No data is sent to our servers — because your commands never pass through servers we control.

Local Communication

The app communicates with the PCLink Agent on your PC through an encrypted WebSocket connection relayed through a secure server. The relay server facilitates the connection but does not store, log, or inspect the content of your commands.

Security Tokens

When you pair your phone with a PC, a unique security token is generated and stored locally on your device. This token is used only to authenticate commands. It is never transmitted to any third party.

Third-Party Services

PCLink does not integrate with any analytics platforms, advertising networks, or third-party data processors. No third-party SDKs that collect user data are included in the app.

Data Storage

All app data — paired devices, custom actions, scheduled events, settings — is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. You can delete this data at any time by removing the app.

Children's Privacy

PCLink is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date above. We encourage you to review it periodically.
